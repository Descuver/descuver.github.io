I just wanted to reach out and apologise for yesterday. I hope I didn’t come across as dismissive as I truly didn’t mean to. I actually think what you’ve been planning is really interesting, and I have no issue with it at all! The truth is, I sometimes struggle with being part of groups, and it’s more about my own social discomfort than anything to do with your ideas. I’m still trying to get more comfortable in social situations, so I can sometimes shy away, even if I’m interested, and I am can only apologise for this fact. I am really sorry.


Please don’t take it personally, and I hope you understand. I really admire what you’re doing, it is very unique and creative and I hope there’ll be other opportunities similar that I could maybe join in on when I can feel more at ease. I really do hope you keep doing what you're doing! And thanks again for being so understanding and cool about it all.


On a lighter note, today was your first "proper" day of working your new job!? Congratulations once again! I hope it is turning out to be everything you wished for and more!
